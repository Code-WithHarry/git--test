Hot Coffee Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity Image
Coffee 2 tablespoons
Milk 1 cup
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Coco powder 2 tablespoons
Coco powder For design

Instructions

  1. Add coffee, milk, sugar, and coco powder to a blender.
  2. Blend until the mixture becomes frothy and well combined.
  3. Pour the coffee into serving glasses.
  4. Top with Coco powder and design on top.
  5. Serve hot coffe and enjoy!
